Chapter 01
The Lying Brain
பொய் சொல்லும் மூளை

You believe you are in control. You believe that when you make a decision — to buy something, say something, feel something — it is you deciding. Consciously. Rationally. Freely.

You are wrong. And the most disturbing part? Your brain already knows this. It just doesn't tell you.

Psychologist Daniel Kahneman spent decades uncovering what he called System 1 and System 2 thinking. System 1 is fast, automatic, emotional. System 2 is slow, deliberate, logical. Here's the uncomfortable truth: you live almost entirely in System 1.

The brain you trust to keep you safe, to make rational choices, to protect your interests — is the very same brain running on…
