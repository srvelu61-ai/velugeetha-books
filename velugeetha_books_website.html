About This Book

Everything You Need to Know About Special Education

This book provides a clear, practical introduction to special education. It explains key concepts, teaching methods, and strategies to support children with diverse learning needs.

Designed for teachers, parents, and students, this guide helps in understanding inclusive education and effective classroom practices — with content in both English and Tamil.

  • Introduction to Special Education
  • Types of Disabilities (Dyslexia, ADHD, Autism)
  • Inclusive Education – Meaning & Benefits
  • Assessment Techniques & Tools
  • Teaching Strategies & Multi-Sensory Methods
  • Classroom Management
  • Case Studies & Practical Applications
  • Assistive Technology
  • Emotional & Social Development
  • Functional Vision Assessment
  • Future of Special Education
